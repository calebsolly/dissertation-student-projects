Featured Project

Beyond Detection

Joseph Hunt, Koyo Fujii, Aly Magassouba, Praminda Caleb-Solly · ICSR 2025

This project explores how connected robotic systems can move beyond passive hospital fall detection toward proactive, personalised assistance.

The system combines privacy-preserving thermal sensing, real-time bed-exit prediction, and coordinated human–robot–robot interaction.

Published Conference Paper

Beyond Detection — Orchestrating Human-Robot-Robot Assistance via an Internet of Robotic Things Paradigm

Authors: Joseph Hunt, Koyo Fujii, Aly Magassouba, Praminda Caleb-Solly

Venue: ICSR 2025 / Springer Nature

This paper presents a proactive fall-mitigation architecture for hospital environments, combining privacy-preserving thermal bed-exit prediction with coordinated socially and physically assistive robots.
